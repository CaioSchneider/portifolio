Meus Projetos

Time

A linear, story-driven stealth game following Paul, a physics student who develops time travel technology.

Time travel works both as gameplay mechanic and storytelling device. The player can rewind 5 seconds with a 5-second cooldown.

Full stealth experience. No combat. Getting caught is not an option.

Find My Way

A psychedelic Metroidvania where the player fights internal demons.

Bosses represent emotions like Loneliness, Anxiety and Doubt. Each victory grants a positive ability based on the emotion.

Footprints

A 2D precision platformer inspired by Celeste and Hollow Knight.

The player starts with a Dash ability, but using it removes the torch, causing the character to freeze.
